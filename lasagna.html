Decription

This lasagna is packed with rich flavors. A savory blend of sweet Italian sausage and lean groud beef creates a flavorful base, while onion and garlic enhance the depth of the taste. The sauce combines crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste, balanced with a touch of sugar for sublte sweetness.

Fresh parsley, dried basil, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper add layers of aromatic flavor. Layered between lasagna noodlesand a creamy of Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese, the dish is baked to perfections. An eggis mixed into ricotta to give the lasagna a rich, creamy texture.
